What Is Investing?



Investing refers to the act of allocating money or resources with the expectation of generating a return or profit in the future. The primary goal of investing is to grow wealth over time by putting money into various assets, such as stocks, bonds, real estate, mutual funds, or businesses.

Investing involves taking calculated risks, as the value of investments can fluctuate based on market conditions and other factors. The potential for higher returns typically comes with a higher level of risk. Different types of investments have varying risk levels and potential returns.
